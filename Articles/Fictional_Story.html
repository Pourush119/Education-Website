Fictional Story by Author's Imagination

I feel like surrounded by science fiction from my very young days. From the teacher's stories to my mother's courageous speeches. Everywhere I feel, I am with fiction all time. I even once had day-dreamed my days as a fictional character. More precisely dreamed of being a fictional superhero like Spiderman, Captain America, Superman, or that giant-red Hulk. But today when i used to think of my Past Fictional beliefs i laugh. But I still feel the idea of being a boy with superpowers was great. To date, sometimes I think of my life as an extraordinary life of a Superhero. I know it seems a little weird for a teen to think so. But sadly, I can't do anything because my society has always made me surround by fiction. I still remember my days when I was 10 and I used to spend my whole day reading those superhero stories. I used to read 6-7 comics a day. I continued reading fictional stories till my nearest library owner told me that I have read all the fictional books they have. Those days my parents didn't allow me to buy comics and today I have a fear of being laughed at by my wife and friends on the thought of buying comics.

Leave my present, let's throw more light on my fictional world that my imaginations had created around me. I always wanted to be Spiderman, sometimes Batman or maybe Tony Stark...Iron-Man. Confused about which superhero to be. But I more loved the idea of all the superheroes which every superhero possesses. I always knew if I had them I would have done various mischiefs. Although I know that if somebody gives me powers then he would surely give me a big list of rules to follow. As with every fictional story, I would definitely not follow them in the first few days but soon will be remembered of my duties as it happens in every story.

Now let's narrate the story I have imagined when I was 11. I had superpowers for one complete month. And a lot happened in that happened. I will tell you what happened. Superpowers I had were: Superspeed like "FLASH", ability to fly like "SUPERMAN" and more importantly ability to do those powerful energy blasts like "CAPTAIN MARVEL". But I didn't get those from "TESSERACT". But I got the capability to do energy blast with the SUPERDURABLE SUIT OF my favorite "IRON-MAN'. Also i had the super strength of "Hulk". First, I definitely wanted to win the race competitions in my school using my Superspeed. I used my Superspeed and won three competitions. Eventually, I got a bad tick on my superhero profile. From there I became a good superhero as every non-boring superhero story says. Hopefully, in mine, there wasn't any AGENT COLSON involved. On a serious note with my superspeed, I caught a gang of 6 thieves which managed to escape from the cops in a fraction of seconds. I also used my powerful energy blast to catch those thieves. Also another time I saved a child falling from the top of LA apartments. Here surely I used my flying superpower. I think my readers must be thinking why I had so accurate powers? Don,t worry they will get the answer. Now I mention the biggest alien attack on my city. There where a superhero who is needed most. Everyone was scared to see those big alien ships and were waiting for their superhero to come and save them. I took a grand entry in the field as I was all set for a "UNIVERSAL FIGHT" with these aliens. I think I forgot to describe alines. They were giant jelly-like brown-black creatures. Some were like mad dogs who didn't care about their lives. I had a great fight with them and thank god I had superdurable "MARK 86" which saved me from their attack to great extent. I won and winning was obvious because that was my fictional story. Some after that heavy attack mighty power has taken my powers back saying - "SOME OTHER TIME BOY"

Even today I feel quite sad thinking why mighty power took powers back from me. But sadder and a little angry about the fact that the might power was my imagination. I always love to think of one of the greatest fictional stuff composed by my imagination. I tell this story to my kids today and they question me why aliens attacked my city only why not on complete Earth?
