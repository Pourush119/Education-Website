Computers

Computer is a man-made machine which faithfully follows man-given orders and shortly give man-wanted results. It has been the latest wonderful invention of science. Today it is being used in many fields.

It may be said that the first computer was made by an intelligent young man Blais Pascal in 1642. He made an instrument with many small wheels. Scientifically, the real computer was made by an English mathematician Charles Babbage in the nineteenth century. With the advent of electronics, the computer made rapid development. Now its use is at the zenith.

Computer may be called scientific wisdom. It does the same work as it is done by the mind in the human body. In fact, it has replaced human minds. It has relieved man of boring jobs of figure work. It is such a machine by which countings and information can be received

Now let's look over some of its uses. Mathematical computer is used for performing mathematical operations . The medical computer diagnosses the disease and suggests even the treatment. At home, computers help the house-wife in her household work. In space programs, they help in big and complex work. In factories, these computers proved very useful. Now the government is using them at railway stations and in other government functioned offices to serve various works. In schools, computers can work as teachers and in offices obedient employees. In fact, no field does not bear the stamp of computers.

Since everything having a good side has a dark side also. Computers too own a dark side. It does the job of many people quickly and accurately. So it increases unemployment also. Secondly, it increases laziness in man. Man has begun to depend on it on a large scale. Shortly man would be nothing without it. There is much scope of misusing computers. Today computer is easily accessible so children and youth remain glued to it for hours. They are found wasting their time either by surfing the internet, enjoying playing games, watching movies and videos on Youtube befooling others by giving them reasons for studying online. Even many times they become irrational and start living in an illusion . It all hinders youth mind.
